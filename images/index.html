Nola Beans

NOLA Beans is one of Lakeview’s home grown businesses. It’s a meeting place, a coffee shop, a casual and healthy dining spot. In short: it’s an extension of the favorite room in your house – our kitchen is your kitchen. Owned and operated by locals, NOLA Beans provides comfort food, exquisite coffee and sublime desserts in a relaxed setting. Drop in anytime! We’re open for business, and we’re eager to serve you. We’re energized about this community. We’re gaga over going's on! And, we’re excited about laying a foundation at the heart of Harrison Ave.
